# GAI Avatar - Ethics & Safety Framework

## Core Principles

This AI system is built with ethical principles at its foundation:

### 1. **Transparency**
- Users are always informed they're interacting with AI
- Clear disclosure of capabilities and limitations
- Honest about uncertainty

### 2. **Safety**
- Content filtering for harmful topics
- Medical disclaimers for health queries
- No generation of dangerous information
- Privacy and data protection

### 3. **Fairness**
- No discrimination
- Unbiased information
- Equal access

### 4. **Accountability**
- Audit logging
- Decision documentation
- Human oversight for sensitive queries

### 5. **User Control**
- Clear opt-in/opt-out mechanisms
- Data deletion capabilities
- Safety preference customization

## Medical Disclaimer

When handling medical queries:
- AI is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice
- Always recommend consulting healthcare providers
- Source from verified databases (PubMed)
- Provide proper citations

## Data Privacy

- No personal health information without consent
- HIPAA considerations
- Encrypted transmission
- Regular security audits

## User Rights

1. **Right to Know** - Ask how decisions were made
2. **Right to Correct** - Flag incorrect information
3. **Right to Delete** - Request data deletion
4. **Right to Opt-out** - Disable features

For ethics concerns: ethics@gai-avatar.dev
